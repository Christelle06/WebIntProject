Bacon Burger




Ingredients

  • 1 bun
  • 1 chopped steak
  • 2 bacon slices
  • 2 cheddar slices
  • salad
  • ketchup, mustard

Instructions


1) Clean the salad, brown the steak

2) Add the bacon slices in the pan

3) Heat the bun in the oven for 5 minutes at 180°C

4) Create your burger with salad, sauces, steak, bacon, and cheddar.

4) Heat it in the oven for 5 minutes at 180°C to melt the cheese.
