Chocolate Muffin




Ingredients

  • 120g of flour
  • 60g of sugar
  • 1 package of yeast
  • 1 egg and 1 white
  • 50g of butter
  • 60g of black chocolate
  • 100g of chocolate chips
  • 4 tea spoons of milk

Instructions


1)Warm the oven at 180°C

2) Melt the butter with the black chocolate.

3) Add the egg and the milk and mix.

4) Add flour, sugar and yeast.

4) Butter the plates and fill it half.

5) Bake for 15 minutes at 180°C
